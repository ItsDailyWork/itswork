About It's Daily WORK

It's Daily Work is a paid engagement service that allows individuals around the world to earn basic income online by performing interactions on Twitter.

A user can begin earning income once their account is verified, by liking, commenting, and retweeting sponsored posts on Twitter.

The service was launched by Digital Currency Daily (DCD) in an effort to bring influencer attention to valuable services, or messages found within the Bitcoin SV ecosystem, while combating misinformation.
